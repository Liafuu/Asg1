Profile

Short Music and their Short Stories.
Amehurashi is an Indie Japanese Band.

Amehurashi (Aplysia) is a creature that is said to "rain when it appears".

It always lives in hiding so that it does not rain around,
drawing a story of people living "in the shadow of someone".

haro/葉露 (Vocals)

Ecology of "Amehurashi"

1. Amehurashi's songs and stories are usually short.

2. Amehurashi sings songs with "feelings that you have accumulated".

3. Amehurashi feeds on "negative emotions" and are poisonous.
